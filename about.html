Who I Am

My name is Wyatt Feaster recently I was a UX/UI designer at Staples. I graduated from Eastern Washington University with a BFA in visual communication design. To me design is not just about making something look pretty, but rather making educated design choices via research and feedback, to create the most user-friendly design possible. Without people, there is no purpose for products, so we should be designing to give people the best possible solutions.
